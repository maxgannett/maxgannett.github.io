Roses, Roses, Roses

There’s a saying that first impressions matter and impacts the way people perceive you. It is perceived critical that when you first meet someone that could make or break your opportunity on jobs, relationships, etc. In that regard, we all feel the pain of that first impression or first data with someone you are seeking a relationship with. So much pondering and overthinking about what is the other person thinking? Do they like me? This could be my husband or Wife? ABC’s The Bachelor and The Bachelorette gives us a look into really how important those fears are in the grand scheme of dating.

Looking at data form seasons 1-21 of The Bachelor and The Bachelorette, provided by FiveThirtyEight’s GitHub, begins to show that picture of does the first impression impact the outcome of your life? Looking at the graph below you can see the left graph shows the contestants that were given the coveted first impression rose in the first week of either The Bachelor or The Bachelorette. The right shows the final week of the show and the contestants that won (w), winning the hearts of the Bachelor or Bachelorette. All the others are eliminated during that week or before. Out of all the contestants, however, only 11% on average make it to the end to fall in love. In suing that the first impression of someone isn’t the end all be all that dictates love.

First Impression Love?

That is true in a lot of senses, job and college, but when it comes to relationships it’s a little different. Love is involved and different parts of the brain activate; from the BBC Future article goes in depth to first impressions. “We tend to match with people whom we deem equal in self-worth, physical attractiveness and popularity.” Alluding to the idea that when we first meet someone, we look for this quality of equal. Although this is a very complex and highly studied area, I’m not a neuroscientist or a sociologist. There are many factors as the BBC Future article point out about other phenomena that play into interactions and love.
